Midpoint Circle Drawing Algorithm

Algorithm

In Mid-Point algorithm we do following :

  • 1. Step 1 − Get the coordinates of the center of the circle and radius, and store them in x, y, and R respectively. Set P=0 and Q=R.

  • 2. Step 1 − Get the coordinates of the center of the circle and radius, and store them in x, y, and R respectively. Set P=0 and Q=R.

  • 3. Step 3 − Repeat through step-8 while X < Y.

  • 3. Step 4 − Call Draw Circle (X, Y, P, Q).

  • Step 5 − Increment the value of P.

  • Step 6 − If D < 0 then D = D + 4P + 6.

  • Step 7 − Else Set Y = Y - 1, D = D + 4(P-Q) + 10.

  • Step 8 − Call Draw Circle (X, Y, P, Q).
